About

Hi there, I am a 2nd year student with a interest in Web Development and Creative Media. I chose this pathway because ever since I could remember I have been intrigued by the evolution of digital technology, whether it be visual effects, game consoles or web development. I have grown up with digital technology at my side and would like to help contribute in the evolution of what is to come. It has taken me awhile to realize that this is my intended pathway and would like to kick start it any way possible!


Under the study of my Bachelor I have been introduced to:
  • Web Development and Interactive - HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Brackets
  • Animation - Maya Software, Rendering, Basic Knowledge
  • Game Technologies - Unity Software, C# Coding, Time Management, Front End and Back End
  • Digital Video Production - Priemere Software, After Effects, Basic Camera Use, Editing
  • Media Studies - Allows you to interpret media texts from a different perspective
